Poster design

As a medium for disseminating information, posters have uni- que media attributes. Based on the functional level of recorded information, posters can be understood as visual “documents” of social history. The visual language of posters at different historical stages is also affected by the social trends and styles of the time. It is not only limited to the functional level, but also design thinking. Intuitive expression integrated with technical means.
